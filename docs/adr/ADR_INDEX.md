## What is an ADR?
An Architecture Decision Record (ADR) captures a single significant architectural decision made by the Technical Steering Committee, along with its context, rationale, and consequences.

- **RFCs** define **WHAT** the protocol is.
- **ADRs** document **WHY** specific architectural choices were made.
